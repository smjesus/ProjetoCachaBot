3. Use of cookies

We do not use cookies. Cookies are small, simple text files that websites store in your browser to save your preferences and settings. For example, when you first access a website that uses cookies, that site sends a text file with your preferences to your browser — such as: the region from which you are accessing, your email address, chosen language, color preferences, or products you might like based on your searches, etc. When you visit the site again, your browser sends this file back to the site, so your settings and preferences are applied automatically without you having to configure everything again (unless you choose to).
